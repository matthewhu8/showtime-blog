About Us

Group Leader Matt and his 4 goons: J dog, Twong, Shri, Kuchma

Welcome to Showtime Slimes Sports, where passion, humor, and storytelling had a baby! We’re five friends united by an unwavering love for everything besides baseball, from buzzer-beaters to underdog triumphs. What started as backyard debates and endless game recaps evolved into this blog—a place to share our takes with the world.

Formerly known as the "Carl Club" we bring diverse perspectives, playful conversation, and genuine fandom to every post. Whether it’s analyzing stats, celebrating unsung heroes, or reliving epic moments, we’re here to ignite the sports conversation. Join us in turning passion into a community. Let’s talk sports!
